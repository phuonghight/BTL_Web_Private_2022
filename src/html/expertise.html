Our process

Discovery

We start by identifying your business and project goals, understanding your market and competitors, defining user needs through analytics and research. This provides us with the necessary insight to design the sitemap and information architecture of the website.
01

Design

We turn the findings from our discovery and strategy sessions into creating a user experience that is right for your target audience. After defining the content structure of the website, we create wireframes to help visualize what the pages will look like. We complete the design stage by adding visual elements, turning those layouts into beautiful and functional interface.
02

Build

From a simple website to enterprise-level WordPress projects, our team of full-stack web developers can handle it all. Our responsive websites are bespoke-built using the latest technology to ensure that they are lean and best tailored to your needs. They are also packed with reusable components that would allow you to freely customize your layouts with any combination of assets. We also build e-commerce sites using Woocommerce and Shopify.
03

Handover

After launching and testing the website for loading speed, we also set up security, backup, SEO tools to ensure that your website is best optimized for organic growth. We also offer helpful how-to videos, documentation and support links to help you take over and manage the website on your own.
